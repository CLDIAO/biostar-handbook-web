Assemblers are also more impenetrable in the way they work than aligners. The N50 is 24!!!! that is # obviously and blatantly wrong. After all the read lenght is 100bp! Why???? # That will stay a mystery I am afraid. # # We need to tune velvetg and figure out the right cutoff. # Basically we are rerunning it and observing what happens # velvetg d31 -cov_cutoff 5 velvetg d31 -cov_cutoff 10 velvetg d31 -cov_cutoff 50 velvetg d31 -cov_cutoff 100 # Whoa! this last one works really well! N50 is 18K Note how above we could hash genomes at different sizes 41, 51 etc and redo the assembly. There is a connection (and tradeoff) between the hash size and the performance of the assembler.
